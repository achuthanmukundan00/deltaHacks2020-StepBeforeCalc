About Team StepB4Calc

 Team StepB4Calc is a team of four second year University of Toronto 
        Electrical and computer engineering (ECE) students. In University of Toronto, mental health 
        issue is recognized as one of the major problems, as many students are suffering with this issue.
        We as a team decided to solve this issue by developing web application using Angular, NodeJS, Firebase,
        and Bootstrap 4 to provide some methods of relief.
