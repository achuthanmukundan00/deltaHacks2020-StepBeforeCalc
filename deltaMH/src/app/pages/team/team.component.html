About Team StepB4Calc

 Team StepB4Calc is a team of four University of Toronto Electrical and
        computer engineering students. In University of Toronto, mental health issue is 
        recognized as one of the major problems, as many students are suffering with this issue.
        We as a team decided to solve this issue by developing web application using Angular, NodeJS,
        Firebase, and Bootstrap 4 to provide several ways of relives.
